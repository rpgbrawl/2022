What is the RPG Brawl

The RPG Brawl is a 1vs1vs1vs1 RPG competition on TrackMania² Stadium. 7 maps were picked to decide the best 32 players out there.

Rules

3 dedicated servers will be opened with the 7 maps spread across them. Then a TA/seeding session will start for a full month.
Your rank will be decided by your average local record on these maps.
The top 32 will be qualified for the playoffs, and will then compete in matches with round mode up to 70 points.
At the start of a match, all 4 drivers will ban a map, and they will play on the remaining 3.
It's a double elimination system, which means there is a loser bracket.

Cashprize

  • 1st - 200€ + a glass trophy
  • 2nd - 120€
  • 3rd - 70€
  • 4th - 40€
